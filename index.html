About Me

I'm a Senior Software Engineer at HashedIn by Deloitte, one of the world's Big 4 professional services firms, where I design and ship distributed backend systems for large enterprise clients across the US and NZ — using Java, Spring Boot, and AWS.

Before Deloitte, I spent two years at Cognizant working on Marriott International's Property Management System, a hospitality platform running across thousands of hotel properties worldwide. My contributions were recognized with a Spotlight Award presented by Marriott's VP of Technology Product Management. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology (GPA 3.9/4.0) and am always looking to grow across cloud architecture, distributed systems, and backend engineering.
